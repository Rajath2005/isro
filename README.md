## 🌕 Introduction

**AI Lunar Mission Planner** is an AI-powered decision support platform designed to assist mission planners in lunar exploration.

The platform enables:

* Detection of subsurface ice deposits
* Identification of optimal landing zones
* Terrain and hazard analysis
* Rover route optimization
* AI-assisted mission blueprint generation

It is inspired by real-world aerospace mission control systems and aims to streamline complex mission planning workflows into a unified interface.

## ⚠️ Problem Statement

Planning lunar missions involves multiple complex challenges:

* 🌑 Unknown and uneven terrain
* 🛬 Safe landing zone identification
* 💧 Detection of water ice resources
* 🔋 Energy optimization for long missions
* 🤖 Autonomous rover navigation
* 🔬 Scientific mission planning and prioritization

---

## 💡 Our Solution

AI Lunar Mission Planner integrates:

* Satellite imagery analysis
* Terrain modeling
* Hazard detection algorithms
* AI-driven decision support

into a **single unified platform** that enables mission planners to:

* Make data-driven decisions
* Reduce mission risks
* Optimize resource utilization
* Improve scientific outcomes
